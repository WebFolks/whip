FAQ

Pre-orders

What does a pre-order get me?

Your pre-order is a refundable deposit that guarantees your place in the queue for a Whip. When you order a Whip, the value of your pre-order will be deducted from any subscription fee.

Can I cancel my pre-order to get a refund?

Yes. Pre-orders are fully refundable at anytime. Email us to cancel your pre-order and receive a full refund. Refunds will show up in your account within 5-10 working days, depending on your bank.

Try Whip

When will Whip be available?

Whip will be available from the second half of 2020. Pre-order to guarantee your Whip from our first release.

Where is Whip available?

Whip will be available in the US and UK initially. Pre-order to guarantee your place when Whip becomes available in your area.

Where can I test ride a Whip?

Whip will be available to test ride in select locations over the next 12 months. Pre-order to be the first to try Whip, or sign up and we'll update you when Whip is available to test ride in your area.

Security

Does my Whip come with a lock?

Yes. Your Whip comes with a U-lock. Use the lock along with Whip’s built in immobiliser, GPS tracking and steering lock to securely park outside.

If my Whip is stolen, will you really replace it?

Yes. If your Whip was secured with the U-lock provided and was stolen, we will replace it.

Safety & performance

How safe is a Whip? Can I ride it on the road?

Yes. A Whip is designed to be ridden on the road and we think you’ll feel confident doing so. Bright lights, indicators, larger wheels and suspension make a Whip safe and comfortable on the road.

Do I need a helmet to ride a Whip?

We strongly recommend that you wear a helmet when using your Whip. Some countries require riders to wear a helmet by law. Check your local laws before riding.

How do you measure Whip’s range?

We measured Whip’s 50 mile (80km) range based on ideal conditions. We use this figure so that you can easily compare it to other ranges quoted by EV manufacturers. However, based on our real world tests we expect a 200lb (91kg) rider to achieve 20-23 mile range in the city. For comparison, Whip uses a 750Wh battery, the Xiaomi M365 Pro a 473Wh battery and the Boosted Rev a 370Wh battery.

Servicing

If my Whip breaks, will you fix it?

Yes. Unlimited servicing is included in your subscription.

How do I get my Whip serviced?

If you require a service, or your Whip has stopped working, we’ll book an appointment for you at your local bike shop. All you have to do is drop off and collect your Whip. We’ll take care of everything in the middle.

What does the unlimited servicing include?

Unlimited servicing covers everything you need throughout the lifetime of your subscription. Brakes, tires, punctures, all parts and the battery are included.

How does the breakdown recovery work?

In the unlikely event that your Whip breaks down*, you’ll get up to $20 Uber credit to get home. Just let us know through the Whip app.

*excludes punctures

Shipping and returns

How do you get my Whip to me when I subscribe?

Your Whip is delivered to your door.

How does the 30-day return work?

If you subscribe and then change your mind, you can return your Whip to us free of charge. We just ask that you do this within 30 days of making your order and pay for the return shipping.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription we will send you everything you need to return your Whip to us. You just pay for the remainder of your subscription period.

If I cancel my subscription and then want to start again, do I have to pay the upfront one-time fee again?

If you choose the '24 month' subscription plan you will not need to pay the upfront one-time fee. However, if you choose the 'cancel anytime' subscription plan you will need to pay the upfront one-time fee.

About

Whip is a micromobility company built for people who value their time and want to enjoy the journey.

With Whip, you get the benefits of owning a personal light electric vehicle without the headache of ownership.

The Whip electric scooter is designed for people who do sub-10 mile trips every day. It’s a standalone mode of transport for the city, affording its rider the freedom of a car, without the limitations of congestion and parking.

Whip’s co-founders Michael and Sami have ridden thousands of miles on scooters. What they found was an absence of great user experience. From the safety and performance through to servicing and security.

By subscribing to Whip, you get a fast, safe electric scooter that can be securely parked outside. You also get unlimited servicing, and if your Whip's stolen and we can't recover it, we'll give you a new one.

Why 'Whip'?

Whip is another word for car.

A Whip provides the convenience and freedom of a car, but without the deadweight that gets you stuck in traffic and costs 5 minutes either side of your journey to look for parking.
